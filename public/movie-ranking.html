Official Top 3 Movie Rankings

Source: Tyda's Mind

Whisper of the Heart

Heartwarming haters to lovers romance movie between a violinist and writer. Featuring the classic song Country Roads.

Scott Pilgrim vs the World

Hilarious movie adaption of the graphic novel series Scott Pilgrim vs the World by Bryan Lee O'Malley. Follow Scott on a journey to defeat the love of his life's 7 evil exes.

Tangled

Fun, family-friendly Disney take on Rapunzel. Watch and sing along while Rapunzel looks for the meaning of the glowing lanterns each year.
